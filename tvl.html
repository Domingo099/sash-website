Advantages of the ICT Strand

The ICT strand in SHS is designed to equip senior high school students with the necessary technical skills to tackle the IT industry. Graduates are expected to be able to do the following after receiving their education:

Utilize industry standard equipment and to think critically and solve problems involving software, hardware, and other digital domains. ELECTRICAL INSTALLATION AND MAINTENANCE (EIM) STRAND



The Objectives of the EIM Strand

Advantages of the EIM Strand

Ensuring safety and compliance: The primary objective of electrical maintenance is to ensure your home and family’s safety. Regular inspections allow electrical maintenance professionals to identify potential hazards, such as faulty wiring, loose connections or outdated components.

Preserving the longevity of your electrical system: Another objective of electrical maintenance is to maximize the life span of your electrical equipment and components. Well-maintained electrical systems tend to operate more efficiently, experience fewer breakdowns and have an extended service life.

Improving energy efficiency: By optimizing your electrical system’s performance, you reduce energy waste and lower your electricity bills. Regular maintenance tasks, such as cleaning air conditioning condenser coils, checking insulation and calibrating thermostats, contribute to improved energy efficiency. Electrical maintenance professionals can also recommend energy-saving upgrades.

Preventing Costly Problems: By scheduling consistent electrical maintenance services, you’ll be able to catch potential areas of repair before they wreak havoc on your system. Most electrical parts are not visible, meaning it’s harder for the average homeowner to keep an eye on the system as a whole.

Protect Appliances: You’ve likely spent a considerable amount of money furnishing your home with the latest and greatest appliances to make your home’s weekly chores a tad bit easier. It’s essential to keep in mind that the efficiency of your appliances will work hand in hand with your home’s electrical system, and skipping preventative maintenance can hinder both.

Lower Energy Consumption: By allowing our trained electricians to take a look at your electrical system, our team will be able to recognize any areas of concern that may be draining more energy than necessary. Our technicians will inspect your system and offer energy-efficient solutions tailored to your home’s needs.
